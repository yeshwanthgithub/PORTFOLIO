ABOUT

Today, we process over 2 billion searches a year for travel information, helping millions of travellers around the globe to make confident decisions. With every query, airline searches hundreds of travel sites to show travellers the information they need to find the right flights, hotels, car rentals and holiday deals. In over a decade, we've grown from a small office of 14 employees into a company of over 1,000 travel-loving teammates working across 7 international brands Cheapflights, Mundi and HotelsCombined. Together, we help people experience the world by creating their favourite travel tools. In 2013, we were acquired by Booking Holdings, the world leader in online travel.

AWARDS
